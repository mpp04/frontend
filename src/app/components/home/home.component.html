Welcome to mpp's Hot Beverage parlour!

My name is MPP and I am a recent Political Science PhD convert into IT. I am trained as a Angular / Java 8 / SpringBoot / Postgresql Full Stack developer and this is my sandbox web application: Hot Bev 2. It is intended to be the online front of an imaginary hot beverage venue, where people can order coffees, teas and hot soups (because I like soups). It is version 2 because I have had to resuscitate the project, having neglected it for a few months. By visiting the Menu > Custom Coffee Drinks, patrons can create a coffee drink of their own, which will be made once they visit the shop. These are bare bones so to get the application running. Without any prior experience in java / angular development whatsoever, this is the result of 2 months of interrupted sprints (I was not solely focused on the project) from late December 2020 to early February 2021. I started from scratch and have attended all elements of the full stack app development, including the CI/CD pipeline. Where credit is due and on occasion I have asked for help on debugging from my friend **lilmilkboi**. This is work in progress. last_update: 15AUG2021
